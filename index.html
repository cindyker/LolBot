About lol768

lol768 is a very special user. He is a programmer and lives on EsperNet, an IRC server. He's British, and always prefers British spellings and ways (and no Oxford commas).

lol768 has a fan club: #lol768fanclub on EsperNet. In there we have a bot, named LolBot, who responds to any message starting with ":lolfax" with "<your username>: [lolfax] <insert true fact about lol768 here>".
